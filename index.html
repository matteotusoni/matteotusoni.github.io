About me

I work on causal inference and statistical modeling for time series, with applications to macroeconomics and finance. My background is in theoretical physics; I like connecting rigorous analysis with empirical signals and building lean tools that actually help us reason about complex systems.

  • Now: PhD researcher (Sapienza); causal discovery for real-world time series.
  • 2025: Applied research at Bank of Italy (macro time series; multi-agent modeling).
  • Previously: Data Science at EY and Leonardo; ATLAS/CERN (Higgs & ML for HEP).
